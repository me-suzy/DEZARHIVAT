 This is my first ever addon. This addon lets you know which news
 recieves the most hits. I will add more features in the next version.
 YOU MUST HAVE THE FULLNEWS ADDON.
 
 SETUP:
  1. Open hothits.cgi and check if the perl path is corrent for your server.
  2. Upload all scripts (npa_hothits.pl, hothits.cgi, and hhdata.db)
     All in ASCII Mode.
  3. CHMOD hothits.cgi to 755 and hhdata.db to 666.
  4. If you ndisplay.pl, replace the full news link to the following..
     <a href="/news/hothits.cgi?action=jump&URL=http://www.yourdomain.com/news/fullnews.cgi?newsid$newsid">
  5. Now, Build news and there ya go! Check your hits at the newspro.cgi link to "Hot Hits".
